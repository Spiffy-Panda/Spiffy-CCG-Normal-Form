# Step 12.3 — Card threat audit (2026-04-20)

**Verdict: closer-density is the dominant cause of the 80 % baseline
draw rate.** Three of five mono-factions — BULWARK, HOLLOW, TIDE —
have **zero `closer` cards**. THORN has ~14 %. Only EMBER (83 %) is at
target. This is a card-pool deficiency, not an AI tuning problem and
not primarily an engine problem (though the engine audit
[`engine-audit-2026-04-20.md`](engine-audit-2026-04-20.md) queues one
knob as a backstop).

Card authoring is scoped to a follow-up session — this audit delivers
the tagging, the ratios, and the per-faction gap list. The step-12.3
commit template ("one card, one commit, with bench") applies to that
follow-up.

## Tagging rules

Per the 12.3 plan:

- **`closer`** — pushes Conduit damage. Direct-damage Maneuvers,
  Force-carrying Units that can realistically Clash Conduits,
  Arena-pressure effects, Fortification-reduction, Pierce-style
  passives.
- **`setup`** — enables closers. Card draw, Aether acceleration,
  tutoring, Banner builders, token / Sprawl / Rally generators that
  eventually project Force, Resonance Field manipulation.
- **`disruption`** — stalls opponent. Removal, bounce, discard,
  taxes, counter effects, Fortification granted to own side,
  heal / Mend, damage prevention.
- **`filler`** — none of the above materially. A vanilla body with
  mediocre stats, or a narrow effect that rarely matters.

Each card takes exactly one tag; borderline cases (e.g., 1-cost
Rally tokens) resolve to the tag representing their primary role.

## Follow-ups — card authoring queue

These are the concrete card-design gaps flagged by the audit. Each
needs its own commit per the 12.3 template; each needs a `make
bench-ai` result attached; and the 12.1 AI-floor iteration loop
re-fires if any card shift moves matched-pair win rates by > 10 pp.

### BULWARK — author ≥ 5 closers (target ≥ 30 % closer)

Design hook: BULWARK's identity is the immovable wall. Closers that
fit the flavour:

1. A mid-cost Unit with `Fortify N` + "While Fortified, this deals
   damage to target Conduit equal to its Fortify count at End of
   Clash" — turns the wall into a siege engine without giving up
   defence.
2. A Maneuver "Retaliation strike": deals damage to target Conduit
   equal to damage prevented this turn. Rewards stacking Prevent /
   Fortify.
3. A Standard "While you control two or more `Sentinel` Units, at
   End of Fall deal 1 damage to target Conduit." Win-con turned on
   by its own defensive density.

### HOLLOW — author ≥ 4 closers (target ≥ 20 % closer)

Design hook: HOLLOW's identity is corroding what the opponent has.
Closers that fit:

1. A 4-cost Maneuver "Erasure": deals damage to target Conduit equal
   to cards in opponent's Cache. Scales off HOLLOW's own Pilfer /
   exile work.
2. A Unit with Phantom + "When this becomes Phantomed, deal 1 damage
   to target Conduit." Phantom triggers now become Conduit
   pressure, not just evasion.

### TIDE — author ≥ 4 closers (target ≥ 20 % closer)

Design hook: TIDE's identity is repositioning. Closers that fit:

1. A Unit "When you Reshape, deal 1 damage to target Conduit in this
   Arena." Reshape subgame now threatens lethal.
2. A Maneuver "Tidebreaker": deals damage to target Conduit equal to
   the number of Units you've Reshaped this game (capped at 5).
   Pays off the deck's existing setup cards.

### THORN — author ≥ 2 closers (target ≥ 20 % closer)

Design hook: Sprawl / Rally payoff that projects real Force.

1. A Unit with `Rally 3` + "While you control three or more Sprawl
   Units, this gains Blitz." Flips tokens from scaffolding into
   damage.
2. A Maneuver "Overgrowth surge": deals damage to target Conduit
   equal to your Sprawl count. Mirrors EMBER's Maneuver-scaling
   pattern in THORN's vocabulary.
