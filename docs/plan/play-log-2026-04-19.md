## Notes for future polish

1. **Driver picks `Target Conduit #67` (already collapsed) on Maneuver
   resolves.** Spark and Smolder's damage wasted. The frontend driver
   should prefer live Conduits. Trivial fix: skip the `#67` Target
   when its integrity is 0.
2. **The `Attack` button re-appears after a single unit's prompt.**
   Each attacker gets its own prompt; the driver hits Attack multiple
   times correctly but logs duplicate entries at the same step. Not
   a bug, just a noisy log.
3. **Tracker shows `Round 1` throughout.** The engine did increment
   the round counter (back-end confirms via `Game.counters.round`
   tracking); the frontend's `renderEngineBanner` reads from
   `buildView(state.gameState).round`, but `state.gameState` is only
   fetched on certain SSE events — not every round tick. The tracker
   needs to re-fetch or the round data needs to stream through as a
   dedicated frame. Logged for a future polish pass.
4. **The game took 40 clicks (including mulligan passes and
   redundant Attack presses).** A fully optimal driver could have
   closed it out in ~18.

spec can replay this exact sequence (just remove the in-between

the `GameEnd(reason=TwoConduitsLost)` in the SSE log. That's 8k.
